New Covenant World Outreach is a 501 (c) 3 non-profit organization with all donations being tax deductible. Our mission in life is according to Matthew 28, to GO YE unto all the nations to make disciples of Jesus Christ. In John 3:3-8 and 1 Peter 1:23 we are commanded to be born-again, for only then shall we see the Kingdom of God. No religion will save us; we can only be saved by the Word of God. We preach Jesus Christ and the Kingdom of God, not Christianity which are the views and opinions of men and religion. In Matthew 25 Jesus said we are to feed the hungry, clothe the poor, help the sick, and take care of widows and orphans. We do such with medical aid and distribute Bibles. We have families living full time in Kenya Africa and the Philippines. We covet your prayers and give thanks to God for your generous heart in helping us reach the nations. We also send support to feed the widows and orphans in India and Pakistan. The Plight of Starvation and famine is now upon us according to Matthew 24 as a sign of the last days. In Kenya alone, 500 die per day of starvation. May the Lord richly bless you in your prayerful support of this ministry.
